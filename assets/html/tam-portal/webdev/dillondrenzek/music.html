> Dates With Eights MUSIC

When I wrote this song, I had just been on a date with a really good looking girl, hence the eight (she was really more of a nine, but that doesn't rhyme as well). I took some inspiration from the song Allein by Pryda. My favorite part about this song is the combination of a classical house, "four-on-the-floor" type of beat, combined with some triplet rhythms in the middle. That pumping of the synths is caused by a technique called sidechaining. Basically, the audio from the kick drum compresses the sound of the synths, so everytime the kick drum hits, the synths are affected. Normally, this effect coincides with the typical four-on-the-floor beat used by a lot of dance music. I switched it up a little in this song to try and give it a bit more groove. Enjoy!
